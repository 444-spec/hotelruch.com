Explore Kampala

The city is yours to discover

Wildlife & Adventure Safaris

Trek gorillas in Bwindi, experience the power of Murchison Falls, or explore Queen Elizabeth and Kidepo National Parks. Visit the source of the Nile in Jinja for white-water rafting, ATV drives, and bungee jumping.

Tour the City

Visit the Bahai Temple, Kasubi Tombs, comedy nights at the National Theatre, and the spectacular Ndere Cultural Centre dinner show.

Craft Markets

Discover handcrafted souvenirs, beads, baskets, and batiks at Buganda Road and the National Theatre — a 5-minute walk away.

Uzuri Gardens

Unwind in our serene wildlife-themed gardens — no noise, no distractions, just you, your thoughts and nature.

Taangavu Restaurant

Finger-licking cuisine from our in-house restaurant — a variety of tasty dishes at quality that rivals any hotel in Kampala.
